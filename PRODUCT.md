# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Roblox game developers and creators who use AI coding agents (Claude, Codex, Antigravity, etc.) to accelerate their scripting, UI creation, terrain building, and scene management workflows in Roblox Studio.

## Product Purpose
Provide a robust, high-performance local bridge and Roblox Studio plugin that facilitates seamless, bidirectional filesystem-to-Studio synchronization, command execution, and AI agent control.

## Brand Personality
- **Studio-Native**: Feels like an official, integral part of Roblox Studio's professional suite.
- **Developer-Centric**: Clear, dense, precise, and transparent about what it is doing.
- **Crafted**: High visual alignment, premium typography, and subtle, tactile details.

## Anti-references
- **SaaS-Cream Clichés**: Soft-beige bgs, massive drop shadows, bloated spacing, and marketing buzzwords.
- **Insanely Rounded corners**: Card radii above 12px that look childish or unsuited for professional tools.
- **Side-stripe borders**: Side-stripes on cards or logs that make it look amateurish.
- **Text overflows**: Large display titles or kickers that break layout.

## Design Principles
- **Respect the Workspace**: Keep spacing tight and high-density, maximizing the visible area for code and logs.
- **Studio-Cohesive Palette**: Seamlessly match the active Studio theme (light or dark mode), ensuring seamless visual integration.
- **Tactile feedback**: Subtle micro-animations and distinct states on interactables (hover, active, disabled) to convey actionability.
- **Functional typography**: Clean fonts, strong weight hierarchy, and clear monospace formatting for code-like information.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios meeting WCAG AA standards (>= 4.5:1 for body and labels).
- Consistent font scale and readability in both Roblox Studio Light and Dark themes.
- Clear visual cues (badge labels like "OK" / "ERR" rather than just color) for color-blind users.
